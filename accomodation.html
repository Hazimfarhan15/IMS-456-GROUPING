Accomodation

Planning for a staycation or holiday?? Here are some of our suggestion.






Luxury

  • Mabul Island Resort
  • Exclusive Mabul resort with 16 units of floating bungalows with private balconies and panoramic vistas of the ocean. Sister resort of Sipadan-Mabul Resort (SMART).
  • RM 2400 Per Night

Mid-Range

  • Mabul Beach Resort
  • This Mabul resort consists of 24 ensuite rooms situated on the beachfront. Scuba Junkie also operates Scuba Junkie Backpackers based in Semporna.
  • RM845 Per Person

Budget

  • Uncle Chang Sipadan
  • Wooden lodge built on stilts over the sea. Dorm, double, family rooms and seaview chalets available.
  • 1 day diving in Sipadan from RM600 (non-divers), RM720 (2 dives) and RM750 (3 dives)
